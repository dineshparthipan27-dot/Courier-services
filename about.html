The Road Travelled

From a small local courier to a global logistics powerhouse. Here is our story.

2010

The Inception

Started in a small 200 sq.ft office with just 3 delivery vans, focusing entirely on same-day local city deliveries.

2015

Going National

Expanded our network across all major tier-1 and tier-2 cities. Launched our first 50,000 sq.ft mega sorting hub.

2020

The Tech Revolution

Introduced real-time GPS tracking, automated sorting robots, and released our B2B API for seamless E-commerce integration.

2026

Global Logistics Leader

Operating in 200+ countries with a massive fleet of 5,000+ vehicles and dedicated cargo planes. The journey continues!
